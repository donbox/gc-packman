Scaffold a new pack directory.

Creates a pack directory with pack.toml and standard subdirectories
(agents/, formulas/, scripts/).

Usage:
  gc packman init <name> [--dir <path>]

Arguments:
  <name>      Pack name
  --dir       Target directory (default: packs/<name>)

Examples:
  gc packman init my-pack
  gc packman init my-pack --dir ./custom-location
