Download a pack to the local cache without installing it.

Fetches the pack from a registered tap into .gc/cache/packs/ but
does not modify city.toml or pack.lock. Use 'gc packman add' to
install a fetched pack into the city.

Usage:
  gc packman fetch <pack> [--version <constraint>]

Examples:
  gc packman fetch fb/foo
  gc packman fetch fb/bar --version "^2.0"
